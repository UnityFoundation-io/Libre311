## Introduction

Libre311 is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you submit service requests through this platform.

## Information We Collect

When you submit a service request, we collect:

- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number (if provided)
- **Service Request Details**: Description, category, location, photos (if uploaded)
- **Location Data**: Address or geographic coordinates of the service request
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, device type (for technical support)
- **Timestamps**: Date and time of request submission

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Process and respond to your service requests
- Communicate with you about your requests
- Improve our services and platform functionality
- Comply with legal obligations and public records requirements
- Generate anonymized reports and statistics

## Information Sharing

Your information may be shared with:

- Municipal departments responsible for addressing your request
- Public records requests (as required by applicable open records laws)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Public Records

Service requests may be subject to public records laws. Certain information (such as your name, request details, and location) may be publicly accessible. Contact information may be redacted in public records releases in accordance with applicable laws.

## Data Retention

We retain service request information for 1 year or as required by applicable records retention policies and legal obligations.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your submitted service requests
- Request corrections to your information
- Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@example.com.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

## Third-Party Services

This platform may use third-party services (such as map providers) that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

## Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on the platform.
